WELCOME

Prince Edward School (Harare) is one of the oldest schools in Harare. In the 125 years that the school has existed, it has been widely regarded as one of the premier schools in Zimbabwe, Southern Africa and the world at large. Over the years, Prince Edward School has gained a reputation for academic, cultural and sporting excellence, producing men of integrity and value who have become widely influential in Zimbabwe, Africa, and the rest of the world. Today the school strives to maintain and even surpass this high standard.
